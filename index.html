Cat Patterns.

Calico cats are almost always female! The unique tri-color pattern is linked to the X chromosome.

Tilapia cats aren't a breed - "tilapia" refers to a coat pattern featuring stripes, dots, or swirling patterns with an "M" marking on the forehead.

Tortoiseshell cats, like calicos, are almost always female due to the genetics of their coat colors. They're known for their distinctive "tortitude" - a feisty, independent personality!

Orange cats are about 80% male! They're also famous on the internet for sharing one brain cell among all of them.
